// WASP48B IS CLOSER TO CONNECTICUT THAN FLORIDA //

This popular myth is about perspective. It is indeed true that can see WASP-48b from a telescope in Connecticut, but you cannot see Florida. However, this does not mean WASP-48b is closer. This is because the earth is flat -- and while you can look up from the earth, you cannot look across it.
